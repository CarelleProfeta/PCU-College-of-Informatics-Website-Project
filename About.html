ADVANCING GLOBAL TECHNOLOGICAL INNOVATION

Welcome to the College of Informatics, the path to thriving careers in computer engineering and information technology.

The academic programs offered by the college strive to educate students, inspire excellence in research and innovation, discover new knowledge, technologies, and systems, and embrace a culture of service.

The college continues to conceive and develop programs to be at part with the advancement of modern global technology. Combined with PCU’s long-standing tradition of providing strong Christian education, the college offers a holistic education.

Integrating research, strong theoretical background, and hands-on experience in laboratories and practical immersions, students are given space to work in teams, develop collaboration, foster cooperation and connections, and engage in relevant and meaningful conversations guided by faculty members who are experts and specialists in their fields.

Believing that technological infrastructure, engineering industry, and research innovation are the cornerstones of our economy, the college endeavors to make meaningful and lasting contributions to the society by producing graduates who are critically aware and responsive to the needs of the times.

MISSION

The College of Engineering and Information Technology is committed to produce engineering and information technology professionals who are:​

  • Fully prepared and equipped to meet the challenges of engineering and information technology
  • Productive and responsible members of society;
  • Proficient in their field and highly motivated to excel;​
  • Grounded in a deep faith in God and a commitment to serve.

VISION

A center of excellence in engineering and information technology.

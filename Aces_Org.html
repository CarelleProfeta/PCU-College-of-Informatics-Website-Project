MISSION

The Association of Computer Engineering Students, commit ourselves to:

  • We help students to understand the importance of communication in effective engineering practice.
  • We collaborate with students, faculty and industry to establish discipline-specific communication instruction.
  • Develop strategies, services and events that allow students to feel more confident.​
  • To educate other students with creative and analysis expertise to be able to address social challenges.
  • To exchange information on today’s technologies to other students.

VISION

Association of Computer Engineering Students is committed to delivering outstanding technological experiences to the students of Philippine Christian University that will inspire them to face and resolve the challenges in a world driven by technology.

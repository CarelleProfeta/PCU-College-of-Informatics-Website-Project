COMPUTER SOCIETY

(ComSoc)

Computer Society is the premier source for information, inspiration, and collaboration in computer science. Connecting members of the Computer Society empowers the people who advance technology by delivering too for individuals at all stages of their professional careers. It helps you to grow as a person by doing the activities that we will give to our members and help them to become more good in computer.

MISSION

We, the Computer Society, commit ourselves in:

  • To promote sense of camaraderie and brotherhood among the College of Informatics students.
  • To adopt such measure as maybe necessary for the education and advancement in the field of information technology.
  • To promote and uphold moral values beneficial to the interest of computer related professions, to oneself and to others and to instill discipline among its members
  • To account the member and the nature of the computer related activities

VISION

We, the members of the Computer Society of Philippine Christian University, imploring the aid of Almighty God, serve asamedium of expression of an ideal aspiration to promote and protect the Welfare and interest of the students of the College of Informatics and strengthen the communities that bind us.
